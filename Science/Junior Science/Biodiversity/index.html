The 3 Levels of Biodiversity

Biodiversity is more than just counting animals. It happens on three scales.

Genetic Diversity

The variety of genes within a species. This is why you look different from your classmates! It helps species adapt to changes in the environment.

Species Diversity

The variety of different species (plants, animals, fungi) in a specific area. Hong Kong has over 3,300 species of vascular plants!

Ecosystem Diversity

The variety of habitats. In HK, we have mangroves, rocky shores, woodlands, and urban parks. Each supports different life forms.

Iconic Hong Kong Species

Fun Fact
Bird

Black-faced Spoonbill

A global endangered species. They migrate to the Mai Po Nature Reserve in Hong Kong every winter to feed in the wetlands.

"They use their spoon-shaped bills to sweep water for fish!"

Fun Fact
Mammal

Chinese White Dolphin

Also known as the Pink Dolphin. Adults are actually pink due to blood vessels near the skin surface regulating body heat.

"They are usually found near Lantau Island."

Fun Fact
Amphibian

Romer's Tree Frog

Endemic to Hong Kong (found nowhere else!). They are tiny, roughly the size of a fingernail.

"Once thought extinct, they were rediscovered in 1984."
